About This Weeks Youth Day

Youth Day, organized by the Birmingham Islamic Society (BIS), is a weekly activity-packed event for children aged 0-13. Every Saturday, we engage in fun, educational, and social activities to foster learning and creativity. This weeks activity is Glow in The Dark Party!!! everyone will get a glow stick(s) and get to play with friends in the dark then we will have a talk about Ramadan and those who lisen will get popsicles!!!
